Discover Manali

1. Mall Road
📍 1.0 KM | Walking / Auto

The bustling heart of Manali. Experience the vibrant pulse of the town, perfect for shopping for local handicrafts, woolens, and souvenirs.

2. Hadimba Devi Temple
📍 2.5 KM | Local Cab / Auto

An architectural and historical marvel. Built in 1553, this unique four-tiered pagoda-style temple is dedicated to Goddess Hadimba.

3. Solang Valley
📍 14 KM | Day-Trip Cab

The adventure and sports capital of Himachal. Solang is famous for unparalleled adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing.
